## 🍔 Swiggy Data Analysis — SQL Case Study

## 📌 Overview
A SQL-based analysis of a Swiggy dataset covering customers, restaurants, orders, and delivery partners using MySQL queries.

## 🔍 Queries Solved

1. Retrieved all customers living in Delhi
2. Calculated the average restaurant rating in Mumbai
3. Listed all customers who have placed at least one order
4. Displayed total orders placed by each customer
5. Found total revenue generated by each restaurant
6. Identified the top 5 restaurants by average rating
7. Found customers who have never placed an order
8. Counted orders per customer in Mumbai
9. Displayed all orders placed in the last 30 days
10. Listed delivery partners who completed more than 1 delivery
11. Found customers who ordered on exactly 3 different days
12. Identified the delivery partner who worked with the most unique customers
13. Found pairs of customers from the same city who ordered at the same restaurant on different dates


## 🛠️ Tools Used
MySQL — JOIN, LEFT JOIN, GROUP BY, HAVING, COUNT, AVG, WITH (CTE), DISTINCT, ORDER BY, LIMIT
